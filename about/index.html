Hello!

My name is Meghan Thommes and I'm currently a PhD Candidate at Boston University! I'm in the Department of Biomedical Engineering and I work for Daniel Segrè. My research focuses on using computational tools to understand and design microbial communities.

I'm defending in the fall of 2019 and am looking for a full-time position as a computational biologist or data scientist. I'd love to continue working on microbial communities, but am also interested in neurology and rare diseases.

I have two dogs, both of which are nine years old and half Pomeranian and half American Eskimo Dog. They are a brother-sister bonded pair that I adopted on Halloween in 2017. Vin (female) was named after a main character in Brandon Sanderson's Mistborn series. Fitz (male) was named after FitzChivalry in Robin Hobb's The Realm of the Elderlings series.
